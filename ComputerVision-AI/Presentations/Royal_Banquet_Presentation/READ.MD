## ğŸ¯ What We Learned  
- Different image formats serve different purposes depending on quality, file size, and transparency needs.  
- Compressed formats like **JPEG** save space but lose detail.  
- Lossless formats like **PNG** keep quality but are larger.  
- **BMP** stores raw image data and is extremely large.  
- **TIFF** is flexible and widely used in professional environments like photography and publishing.  
- Presenting technical information creatively helps make concepts easier to understand.
